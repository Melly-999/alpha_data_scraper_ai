# Course and Certification Roadmap

**Candidate:** Mateusz Ozimkiewicz  
**Goal:** Build verifiable credentials that support target roles in AI automation, technical support, junior backend, and fintech operations.  
**Strategy:** Prioritise free or low-cost certificates with genuine recruiter recognition. Do not collect certificates for their own sake — each certificate should directly support a target job application or portfolio project.

---

## Priority Definitions

| Priority | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **P0** | Do this first. Highest ROI for current target roles. Maximum 2 weeks. |
| **P1** | Do next after P0 complete. Strong recruiter recognition. 1–4 weeks each. |
| **P2** | Medium-term. Builds portfolio depth. 4–8 weeks. |
| **P3** | Long-term optional. Supports senior growth path or specific job requirements. |

## What Not To Do

- Do not start AWS before P0 and P1 are complete. The exam cost is not justified until the junior-role pipeline is active.
- Do not collect certificates without linking each one to a portfolio project.
- Do not list a certificate until it is actually earned.
- Do not spend more than $50/month on paid platforms. Most high-value certificates are free or near-free.
