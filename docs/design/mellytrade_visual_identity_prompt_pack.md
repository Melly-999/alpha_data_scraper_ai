# MellyTrade Visual Identity Prompt Pack

Ready-to-copy prompts for generating MellyTrade brand assets that follow the
approved visual identity board (see
[mellytrade_visual_identity_board_summary.md](mellytrade_visual_identity_board_summary.md)).

Every prompt preserves these invariants:

- Serious B2B fintech / AI terminal style — institutional, restrained, technical.
- Dark terminal aesthetic: deep background `#070A0D`, charcoal panels
  `#10151B` / `#161C23`, mascot orange `#F27A4D`, premium amber `#D6A84F`.
- Read-only / paper sandbox / AI workspace tone.
- Melly Pet is secondary/community only — never the primary official logo.
- Prohibited in all outputs: buy/sell/order/execute controls, broker
  execution, live-trading UX, profit promises, casino/gambling visuals,
  crypto hype.

---

## 1. Official logo — MT pixel monogram + MellyTrade wordmark

```text
Design the official MellyTrade logo: a square pixel-grid "MT" monogram tile
on a deep black background (#070A0D) with a thin amber/orange base accent
(#F27A4D to #D6A84F), paired with a sharp modern "MellyTrade" wordmark in a
geometric grotesque typeface (Space Grotesk / Sora style), white (#F4F6F8)
on dark. Serious B2B fintech / AI terminal brand, institutional and
restrained. Small monospace sub-label "INSTITUTIONAL AI WORKSTATION" in
amber. No mascot character, no faces, no candles-go-up hype imagery, no
buy/sell/order/execute elements, no profit claims, no gambling visuals.
Flat vector style, crisp pixel geometry, dark institutional terminal
aesthetic.
```

## 2. Favicon / app icon — simplified MT monogram

```text
Design a minimal favicon/app icon: a simplified pixel-block "MT" monogram,
white letterforms on a deep black square (#070A0D) with one restrained
orange accent edge (#F27A4D). Must stay legible at 16, 32, and 64 px. No
text besides the MT letterforms, no mascot, no gradients-heavy effects, no
trading-chart hype, no buy/sell symbols. Flat, square-pixel geometry,
serious fintech terminal tone.
```

## 3. Discord icon — Melly Pet head / upper-body only

```text
Design a circular-safe Discord avatar of Melly Pet, the MellyTrade community
mascot: a friendly pixel-art character head and upper body, orange pixel
head (#F27A4D), black suit with charcoal rim-light edges (#2A313A) over a
dark background (#070A0D). Professional "calm market analyst" personality,
not a toy and not a hype mascot. Crop above the waist, keep the suit
```
