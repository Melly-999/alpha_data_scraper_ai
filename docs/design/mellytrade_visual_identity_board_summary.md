## Approved identity hierarchy

| Role | Identity |
| --- | --- |
| Primary official identity | MT pixel monogram + MellyTrade wordmark |
| Secondary identity | Terminal badge / subtle mascot reference |
| Community identity | Melly Pet head / upper-body mascot |
| Product visuals | Dark terminal AI assistant style |

### Mascot rule

Melly Pet is **secondary/community only** — it is never used as the primary
official logo. The official mark is always the MT pixel monogram plus the
MellyTrade wordmark. The mascot may appear as a small companion badge or on
community surfaces, with no cute facial emphasis in official headers or
product chrome.

## Visual style anchors

- Dark institutional terminal palette: deep background `#070A0D`, terminal
  charcoal `#10151B`, panel charcoal `#161C23`.
- Mascot orange `#F27A4D` / soft orange `#FF9A6A` reserved for the monogram
  accent, mascot system, and one focal highlight.
- Premium amber `#D6A84F` / warm gold `#E1B45A` as the safety accent.
- Wordmark typography: Space Grotesk / Sora / Geist. Terminal labels:
  JetBrains Mono / IBM Plex Mono. UI supporting text: Inter / system UI.
- Pixel styling belongs to the MT monogram and mascot system, not body text.
- Safety posture is shown with calm terminal badges:
  READ ONLY / PAPER SANDBOX / AI WORKSPACE / LIVE ORDERS BLOCKED.

## Safety / prohibited visuals

The following are prohibited in all brand and product visuals (these are
brand prohibitions, not features):

- No buy/sell/order/execute controls
- No broker execution
- No connect-live prompts
- No profit promises
- No casino/gambling visuals
- No crypto hype
- No secrets/account IDs
